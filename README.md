Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment

Primetrade.ai Round-0 Assignment

1. Objective

The goal of this analysis is to understand how market sentiment influences trader behavior and performance on Hyperliquid. We want to know if the way people feel about the market affects how traders do. We used the Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index and trader activity data to see if sentiment affects how much money traders make how often they trade and how risk they take.

2. Methodology

We used two sets of data:

- The Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index, which shows how people feel about the market each day.

- Hyperliquid trader data, which shows what trades each trader made including the price, size. If they made or lost money.

We got the data ready by

- changing the timestamps to the date

- combining the sentiment data with the trade data for each day

- creating new metrics to measure trader behavior, such as

How much money each trader made each day

How often they won

How big their trades were

How often they traded

If they traded more often when prices were going up or down

We also used a special math trick to make the financial numbers more stable.

Then we looked at the data to see how trader performance changed when people felt different about the market. We used

- statistics

- pictures to help us see the data

- special tests to compare the data

We also grouped traders by their behavior to see if there were different types of traders.

3. Key Insights

1. Traders do better when people are scared.

When the market sentiment is Fear or Extreme Fear traders make money. This means that good traders often do the opposite of what most people do they buy when people are scared.

2. When people are greedy traders trade more. Make less money.

During Greed periods traders trade often and make more trades but they do not win as much and make less money. This means that when people are too excited about the market it gets crowded and it is harder to make money.

3. When people feel strongly about the market we can see which traders are really good.

When the market sentiment is Fear or Extreme Fear the good traders make a lot money but the not-so-good traders lose more money.

4. Traders who trade a lot are hurt by fees.

Traders who make a lot of trades do a lot of business. They often make less money because they have to pay a lot of fees.

4. Trader Segmentation

We found three types of traders:

- High Size Traders, who make trades and can make or lose a lot of money

- Frequent Traders, who trade a lot but do not make as much money because of fees

- Consistent Winners, who trade often and make good money

These groups of traders do better or worse depending on the market sentiment.

5. Strategy Recommendations

Rule 1. Trade when people are scared if you are a good trader

Good traders make more money when people are scared. They can trade more during these times.

Rule 2. Trade less when people are greedy

When people are too excited, about the market traders should trade less. Be more careful.
